Easy Homemade Pizza Dough

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim – and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

Learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough, and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • .25 oz yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2.5 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, grease a pan, and preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Mix water, yeast, and sugar until creamy
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to yeast mixture. Beat until smooth
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Roll into a 12-inch circle on lightly floured surface
  6. Transfer to greased pizza pan
  7. Add sauce and toppings
  8. Bake for 15 - 20 minutes
  9. Cool for 5 minutes before serving
