Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Description

This turkey pumpkin chili is absolutely delicious. It's quick and easy, too!

This is the perfect meal for chilly fall days when you don't have a lot of time or energy to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • Half cup chopped green bell pepper
  • Half cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1.5 tablespoons chili powder to taste
  • Half teaspoon ground pepper black
  • Dash of salt
  • Half cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Half cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large skilled over medium heat
  2. Add onion, bell peppers, and garlic - stir until tender
  3. Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown, then drain
  4. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin, season with chili powder, pepper, and salt
  5. Reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer 15 - 20 minutes
  6. Serve topped with cheese and sour cream
