Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti is always a hit! A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and everyone loved it. Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests.

I have made this also without the meat, and it is well received.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 2 26 oz jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 oz provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1.5 cups sour cream
  • 6 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil slightly salted water, add pasta, and cook until al dente
  2. Brown ground beef and onion over medium heat, simmer for 15 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and butter a 9x13 baking dish
  4. Spread half of the ziti in the bottom of a prepared dish
  5. Top with provolone cheese
  6. Top with sour cream
  7. Add half of meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce
  8. Top with grated parmesan cheese
  9. Bake until cheese is melted, about 30 minutes
